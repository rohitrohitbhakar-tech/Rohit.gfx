Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get my thumbnails?

+

Our standard delivery time is 2-3 days. However, we offer a 24-hour express service for urgent projects at an additional 30% fee.

What information do you need to create my thumbnails?

+

We'll need your video title, any specific imagery you want included, your preferred style (bright/colorful/minimalist etc.), and your channel branding guidelines if available.

Do you offer revisions?

+

Yes! We include 2 rounds of revisions in all our packages to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final design.

Can you match my existing channel branding?

+

Absolutely. We'll study your current branding and create thumbnails that maintain visual consistency while improving CTR.

What file format will I receive?

+

You'll receive high-quality PNG files (1280x720px) optimized for YouTube, plus the original PSD file if you request it.
